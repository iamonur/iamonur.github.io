A brief summary

  • The work is on the measurement of difficulty and using DDA(Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment) with respect to player's skills.
  • Difficulty is defcined as probability of losing, given a player and a level.
  • They created their own shooting game and 10 different levels with different difficulties that are differing with number of enemies and the skill it takes to kill them.
  • After, they have conducted a user study to test their study.
