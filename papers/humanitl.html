A brief summary

  • Abstract
    • Evaluation of the content is a challange for PCG.
    • This study uses both computational metrics, and surveys.
  • Introduction
    • PCG can generate vast amounts of content, but if the content needs human provision, effectiveness drops.
    • In human-in-the-loop approach, non-expert people are used for evaluation of the content.
    • Human computation evaluates joy, aesthetics, and difficulty.
    • Infinite Mario Bros levels are evaluated by people.
  • Human Computation Approach to PCG
    • A PCG system creates level segments.
    • Workers evaluate segments by three measures.
    • Segments are combined using a mathematical progression model to generate nice-flowing levels.
  • Likert scales 1 to 9 are used for three measures.
