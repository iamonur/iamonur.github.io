A brief summary

  • Abstract
    • Pac-Man levels generated at specific difficulties with respect to number of intersections and elements given.
    • Also used an aesthetic score to check if the maze is Pac-Man worthy.
  • Introduction
    • 99% of the levels produced are playable.
    • 40ms generation time.
  • Methodology
    1. Metrics for a Pac-Man game
      • Ghost house
      • Maze
      • Pills & Power Pills
      • Positioning of the Sprites
    2. Framework
    3. Difficulty vs Intersections
    4. Maze Generation
      1. Grid Preperation: 31x29 grid, same as the original Ms. Pac-Man.
      2. Left Graph
        • Place the ghost house
        • Place the walls, with height of 2 or 3. Left first, up then, low at the end.
        • Eliminate adjacent nodes. Two nodes, and no edge between them => merge two nodes.
      3. Grow From Intersections
        1. Grow from ghost house's intersections.
        2. Grow from cornet intersections.
        3. Uniformly choose from remaining intersections to grow from.
        4. Set pac-man starting point.
      4. Connecting intersections
        • Ignore tunnel grown from ghost house.
        • Connect tunnels and intersections.
        • Connect dead ends.
        • Repeat until left graph is fully connected.
      5. Connect remaining dead ends.
      6. Setup Portals
        • If dead ends can be connected to the left-most border,do it and place a portal at the end.
        • If no portal can be placed, randomly grow a node to generate at least one.
      7. Maze Refinement: Reduce 2x2 walls as much as possible.
      8. Setup pills & power pills
      9. Mirror maze to right.
      10. Setup important locations.
    5. Quality Assurance
      1. Difficulty Evaluation Diff = Weighted sum of (# of Risk tunnels, # of Tunnels, # of Intersections, # of T-Intersections, and # of Pills with Maze size.)
      2. Aesthetic Score Aest = Weighted sum of (Maze area, # of corridors, st. dev. of the block size, st. dev. of the distance between intersections, st. dev. of the distribution of intersections, st. dev. of the distribution of T-Intersections.)
