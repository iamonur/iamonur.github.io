A brief summary

  • Abstract
    • A MxN gray image turned into Mx8N binary image.
    • Then binary image is manipulated bit-level by 2-D cellular automata.
  • Introduction
    • Image Scrambling: Transforming a meaningful image to gibberish.
  • Cellular Automaton
    • 2D-Cellular Automaton
      • Either von Neumann neighborhood is used, or Moore.
      • Boundaries are either zeroes, or randomly chosen.
    • Life-like Cellular Automaton
      • A 2D-CA that is presented as Bx/Sy.
      • Cells are either alive or dead.
      • Bx/Sy = A cell gives birth if x neighbors are alive. A living cell with living neighbors of y means survival. Otherwise, it will die.
      • B3/S1234 = You know what.
