A brief summary

  • Abstract
    • Psychometric Function: Relation between a physical measure of a stimulus and the probability of a particular psychophsical response.
  • Assumptions
    1. The psychometric function has the same shape under all conditions when expressed as a function of logaritmic intensity. From condition to condition, it defers only after a critical value, named T.
    2. The parameter T does not vary trial to trial.
    3. Individual trials are statistically independent.
